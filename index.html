Sandwich Quest

Sandwich Quest is a single page application built by a team of 5 with connecting hungry people to local restaurants. This app uses Yelp, Google Maps, user location data, and a custom ExpressJS API. Our team used Agile principles to organize, plan and communicate about the project. I took the role of product owner, and guided feature and user story decisions through the build process.

Keepr is a Vue 3 SPA with the simple goal of sharing pictures and letting other users "keep" them in their collections. Written with a C# .NET Framework backend, AUTH0 integration, and Dapper.
